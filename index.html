The problem

The Student Experience Is Scattered

Right now, being a student means piecing your academic life together from a dozen different places — and hoping you didn't miss something important.

Missing announcements

Important notices get buried in WhatsApp groups and lecturer emails nobody opens until it's too late.

Hard-to-find resources

Course materials are scattered across drives, group chats, and whichever senior student still has the PDF.

Lost opportunities

Scholarships, internships, and competitions come and go before most students even hear about them.

Poor organization

No single place to track deadlines, results, or what's actually due this week — so things slip.

The solution

Meet StudentPal

One platform, built around how AAUA students actually study, search, and stay updated.

Resource Hub

Course materials organized by department and level, so you find the right note in seconds — not after scrolling forty messages back.

Student Dashboard

A personalized overview of your academic life — saved resources, deadlines, and updates, all on one screen.

Opportunities Board

Scholarships, internships, and competitions, verified and posted in one feed — nothing slips past you again.

Smart Search

Type a course code, a topic, or a lecturer's name — Smart Search finds the resource or update you mean, instantly.

Campus Updates

Official announcements, verified and timestamped — so you always know what's real and what's a rumor.

Student Profile

Your academic identity in one place — department, level, saved resources, and applied opportunities.
